# Product Requirements (PRD)

## Vision
A daily-life assistant centered on parking, extended with news, food suggestions, reminders, and spiritual encouragement, with Cantonese-first voice UX.

## Users
- Drivers in Toronto
- Cantonese/English bilingual users
- Busy professionals

## Core Use Cases
1. Save parking → set timer → get alerts → find car
2. Ask: “Where is my car?” / “How much time left?”
3. Play 10-min Cantonese news (Toronto → Canada → Finance → Tech)
4. Ask: “What should I eat?” (health-based suggestions)
5. Daily spiritual quote (Proverbs/Psalms/Walk-with-God)
6. Manage daily reminders

## Non-Goals (MVP)
- Full Bible offline bundle (license pending)
- Continuous always-on listening

## KPIs
- Daily active usage
- Successful parking reminder completion
- News playback completion rate
- Voice command success rate
